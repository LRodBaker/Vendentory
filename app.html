📋 Physical Inventory Count
Count every product on your shelves. The app compares your count to the book quantity and writes off any difference as shrinkage. Always set the count date before you start.
Scan or search each product, enter your counted quantity, and the app will write off any missing units as shrinkage — with a cost summary ready for your bookkeeper.

⚙ Inventory Costing
WAC (Weighted Average Cost) is recalculated automatically when you receive purchases. Run this manually only if you suspect costs are wrong — it replays every receipt from the beginning.
Recalculates the Weighted Average Cost (WAC) for every active product by replaying all purchase receipts from oldest to newest. Use this to fix any products with incorrect costs that haven't been touched by a recent purchase.
🔍 Inventory Audit
Checks every product for duplicate receipts, impossible quantities, and transaction mismatches. Run this before a physical count to make sure your books are clean.
Scans every product's transaction history for duplicate receipts, impossible quantities, and mismatches between the DB sum and what's shown on-hand. Run this before any physical count.
